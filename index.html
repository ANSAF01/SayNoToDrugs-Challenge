OUR MISSION

Creating a Drug-Free Kerala

The Kerala Police Anti-Narcotics Special Action Wing is committed to eliminating drug abuse through a comprehensive approach that combines enforcement, education, and rehabilitation.

Our mission is to:

  • Disrupt drug supply chains through intelligence-led operations
  • Educate youth and communities about the dangers of substance abuse
  • Provide support and rehabilitation for those affected by addiction
  • Empower citizens to report drug-related activities anonymously
  • Collaborate with schools, colleges, and community organizations

We believe that through collective action, we can protect our youth and build a healthier, drug-free society.
